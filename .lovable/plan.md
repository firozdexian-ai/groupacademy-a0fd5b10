

# Global Product Standard

## Core Rule
**All curriculum and AI instructor personas must be globally positioned** — no region-specific universities, companies, or cultural references unless the program explicitly serves a regional market. Instructors should have international credentials and diverse global experience.

## Curriculum Standard
- 14 courses per program: 5 Foundation, 5 Intermediate, 4 Executive
- ~60 modules total with talking points in descriptions
- All content globally relevant
